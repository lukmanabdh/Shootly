The Problem

Your phone camera can't capture what you see.

Standard smartphone cameras struggle with interior real estate shots. Expose for the window — the room goes dark. Expose for the room — the windows blow out. The result looks unprofessional, undersells every property, and costs you bookings.

Exposed for the window
The room looks like a cave

Walls, furniture, and floors go dark and muddy. The space looks smaller and less inviting than it really is. No amount of brightness adjustment in post will recover the lost detail.

Exposed for the room
Windows blow out to white

Natural light disappears completely. Any garden, street view, or outdoor space is lost. The photo looks flat, artificial, and nothing like what the room actually feels like in person.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for interior real estate photography on Android — not a general-purpose camera app.

AI-powered HDR enhancement

Your exposures are processed by a specialist real estate photography AI. It merges and enhances the image to look bright and natural — not over-processed, not artificially saturated, not fake. This is the same quality of enhancement that previously required Lightroom on a desktop, now done automatically on your phone in under a minute.

Automatic exposure capture

Shootly handles the entire bracketing process automatically. Open the app, point at the room, and hold steady. The app fires multiple exposures and uploads them without any manual shutter speed adjustments, no tripod, and no technical setup. It works on any Android phone that supports the Camera2 API.

MLS compliant results

The AI enhances what is already in the room. It does not add furniture, remove objects, change wall colors, or modify anything physical about the space. Every result meets standard MLS photo submission guidelines, making it safe to use directly for property listings.
